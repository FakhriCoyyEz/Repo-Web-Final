About Me

Reyzidane

Web & Game Developer

I’m Reyzidane, a 16-year-old Web Developer and Game Developer who loves creating interactive digital experiences. I specialize in front-end development, building responsive interfaces and clean user experiences using modern tools and best practices. Outside of coding, I also enjoy music—especially rap—and Travis Scott is one of the artists who inspires my creative energy.

Beyond web development, I explore game creation and enjoy building custom Minecraft mods that blend creativity, code, and gameplay design. Working on mods has taught me problem-solving, scripting, asset creation, and how to turn ideas into features that players genuinely enjoy.

I’m constantly improving myself—learning new technologies, experimenting with design, and building projects that challenge me to grow. My goal is to create digital products and game experiences that are functional, polished, and meaningful for users.
